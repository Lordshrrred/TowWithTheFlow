About

The EarthStar Initiative

Vibration of Awesome helps people unlock themselves. EarthStar is what becomes possible when enough people do.

The self realization of the individual is the seed of planetary transformation.

These are not separate ideas. They are the same movement at two different scales. The inner work comes first. Once you have built something real inside yourself, the natural impulse is to bring it outward.

To the people around you. To the communities that need it. To the planet that sustains all of us.

EarthStar is that impulse, organized into a vision.
