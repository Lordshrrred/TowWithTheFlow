> **Quick Answer:** Weekend towing in Phoenix runs $125-200 for the hook-up, plus $3-5 per mile. Expect 25-50% higher rates than weekdays. Long-distance tows (over 10 miles) can hit $300-500 total. Call multiple companies for quotes.

## What To Do

1. **Get multiple quotes immediately** - Prices vary wildly between companies, especially on weekends
2. **Ask for the total breakdown** - Hook-up fee, per-mile rate, and any weekend surcharges
3. **Confirm your exact location** - Phoenix metro is huge, and distance affects your final bill
4. **Check if your insurance covers towing** - Many policies include roadside assistance that covers weekend rates
5. **Ask about payment methods** - Some companies charge extra for card payments on weekends
6. **Get arrival time estimate** - Weekend demand means longer waits, sometimes 2-3 hours

## What It Might Cost

**Base weekend rates in Phoenix:**
- Hook-up fee: $125-200 (vs $85-150 weekdays)
- Per-mile rate: $3-5 (same as weekdays)
- Weekend surcharge: $25-50 additional
- After-hours fee (nights): $50-75 extra

**Real examples:**
- Short tow (5 miles): $150-225
- Medium tow (15 miles): $200-300  
- Long tow (25+ miles): $300-500

**Premium services cost more:**
- Flatbed vs wheel-lift: Add $25-50
- Motorcycle towing: Add $50-100
- Heavy-duty (trucks/RVs): $200-400 base rate

## Stay Safe

- Stay with your vehicle if safely possible
- Use hazard lights and reflective triangles
- Don't accept rides from unlicensed tow operators
- Verify the company name matches what you called
- Take photos of your car before it gets hooked up
- Get a written receipt with all charges listed
