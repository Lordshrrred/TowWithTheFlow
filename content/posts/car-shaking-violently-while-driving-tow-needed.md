> **Quick Answer:** Pull over immediately and stop driving. Violent shaking while driving indicates serious mechanical failure that can cause accidents or total breakdown. Common causes include wheel separation, broken axles, or severe engine problems. Call for a tow truck right away.

## What To Do

1. **Pull over safely immediately** - Use hazard lights and get to the shoulder or parking lot as quickly as possible
2. **Turn off the engine** - Stop all movement to prevent further damage
3. **Exit the vehicle carefully** - Move away from traffic if on a roadway
4. **Check for obvious damage** - Look for loose wheels, visible fluid leaks, or hanging parts
5. **Call for roadside assistance** - Do not attempt to drive further
6. **Wait in a safe location** - Stay away from the vehicle if near traffic

## What It Might Cost

Towing typically runs $75-200 depending on distance and location. Emergency roadside service may add $50-100. The underlying repair costs vary dramatically:

- Wheel bearing failure: $200-500
- CV joint replacement: $300-600  
- Engine mount failure: $200-800
- Transmission problems: $1,500-4,000+

## Stay Safe

• Never ignore violent shaking - it indicates imminent failure
• Do not drive even short distances to a repair shop
• Keep passengers away from the vehicle until help arrives
• If on a highway, exit the vehicle from the side away from traffic
• Use flares or reflective triangles if you have them
• Stay visible to approaching traffic while waiting for help
