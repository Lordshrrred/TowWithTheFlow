> **Quick Answer:** Towing luxury cars like Mercedes and BMW costs $150-400+ compared to $75-200 for standard vehicles. Premium cars require flatbed transport, specialized equipment, and experienced operators. Distance, location, and specific model affect final pricing.

## What To Do

1. **Call specialized luxury towing services first** - not regular tow companies that might damage your vehicle
2. **Request flatbed towing only** - wheel-lift or dolly towing can damage AWD systems, air suspension, or low ground clearance components
3. **Verify insurance coverage** - check if your auto insurance, AAA premium, or manufacturer roadside assistance covers luxury vehicle towing
4. **Confirm operator experience** - ask if they regularly handle your specific make and have proper equipment for low-profile vehicles
5. **Document everything** - take photos before towing and get written confirmation of pickup/delivery locations
6. **Remove valuable items** - luxury cars often contain expensive electronics, accessories, or personal items

## What It Might Cost

**Local towing (under 10 miles):**
- Standard tow: $150-250
- Emergency/after-hours: $200-350

**Long-distance towing:**
- $3-7 per mile after initial hookup fee
- Cross-country transport: $1,200-2,500+

**Premium factors that increase cost:**
- Flatbed requirement: +$50-100
- Low ground clearance: +$25-75
- All-wheel drive precautions: +$25-50
- Exotic/supercar handling: +$100-300

## Stay Safe

- Never let operators use wheel-lift towing on AWD vehicles
- Confirm tow truck has proper ground clearance for your car's approach angle
- Insist on soft straps instead of chains for tie-downs
- Verify destination address twice before signing paperwork
- Keep spare key accessible if car has electronic locks that might fail
