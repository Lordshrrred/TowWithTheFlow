> **Quick Answer:** GEICO roadside assistance costs $14-25 per year and covers towing up to 15 miles. Direct tow truck calls cost $75-150 for the same distance. If you need towing more than once every 3-5 years, GEICO's plan saves money.

## What To Do

1. **Check if you already have GEICO roadside assistance** on your policy. Look at your declarations page or call your agent.

2. **Compare immediate costs:**
   - GEICO roadside: Free service calls if you have coverage
   - Direct tow truck: $75-150 for local towing, $3-7 per mile beyond

3. **For emergency situations without coverage:**
   - Call local tow companies for quotes
   - Ask about cash discounts (often 10-15% off)
   - Verify mileage charges upfront

4. **Add GEICO roadside assistance** if you don't have it and your car is older than 5 years or you drive frequently.

## GEICO Roadside Assistance Coverage

**Basic Plan ($14-19/year):**
- Towing up to 15 miles
- Jump starts, flat tire changes
- Lockout service
- Emergency fuel delivery

**Enhanced Plan ($25-30/year):**
- Towing up to 100 miles
- Same services as basic
- Trip interruption benefits

## Direct Tow Truck Costs

**Typical pricing:**
- Base fee: $50-100
- Per mile: $3-7 after first few miles
- After hours: Add 25-50%
- Weekends/holidays: Add $20-40

**Example:** 10-mile tow costs $85-170 depending on company and time.

## When GEICO Makes Sense

- You drive an older vehicle (5+ years)
- You commute long distances daily
- You've needed roadside help before
- Peace of mind is worth $1-2 monthly

## When Direct Payment Works Better

- Newer reliable vehicle under warranty
- Rarely drive or short local trips only
- Good emergency fund available
- Live in area with competitive tow pricing
