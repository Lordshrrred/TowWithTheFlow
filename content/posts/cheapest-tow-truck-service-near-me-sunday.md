> **Quick Answer:** Sunday tow rates run $75-150+ due to weekend premiums. Call multiple local operators, check AAA membership, search "24/7 tow" on Google Maps, and avoid highway predators who charge $200-400. Independent shops often beat big chains on Sunday pricing.

## What To Do

1. **Search Google Maps for "24/7 tow truck"** - Filter by currently open, call at least 3 shops
2. **Check your insurance roadside coverage** - Many policies include free towing up to 15 miles
3. **Call AAA if you're a member** - Sunday rates same as weekdays, typically $50-100 total
4. **Ask for flat rate pricing upfront** - Get mileage rate and any Sunday surcharges in writing
5. **Try independent operators first** - Small local shops often charge less than big chains
6. **Avoid highway "vultures"** - Don't accept unsolicited help from trucks that just show up
7. **Get pickup and destination addresses confirmed** - Prevents surprise mileage charges

## Stay Safe

- Stay in your vehicle if on busy roads
- Turn hazards on, park as far right as possible
- Never leave vehicle unattended with valuables visible
- Verify tow truck company name and driver ID before getting in
- Keep phone charged for multiple calls
- Have cash ready - some Sunday operators prefer it
- Take photos of your car's condition before towing
