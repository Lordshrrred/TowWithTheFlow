> **Quick Answer:** After hours towing in Phoenix typically costs $150-300 base rate plus $3-7 per mile. Weekend and holiday rates run highest. Expect 20-50% surcharges over daytime rates. Call multiple companies for quotes since prices vary significantly.

## What To Do

1. **Call your insurance first** - Many policies include roadside assistance that covers towing up to certain limits
2. **Get quotes from 3-4 companies** - Prices vary wildly, especially after hours
3. **Ask about total cost upfront** - Base rate plus mileage plus any surcharges
4. **Confirm your exact location** - Use GPS coordinates or nearby landmarks
5. **Ask for estimated arrival time** - After hours service can take 45-90 minutes
6. **Have payment ready** - Most require credit card or cash on scene
7. **Take photos** if accident-related - Before tow truck moves your vehicle

## What It Might Cost

**Base Rates (After Hours):**
- Weekday nights: $150-250
- Weekends: $175-275
- Holidays: $200-300+

**Additional Fees:**
- Mileage: $3-7 per mile
- Winching: $75-150
- Flatbed upgrade: $25-75
- Storage per day: $25-45

**Example:** 15-mile tow at 2 AM on Saturday might cost $225 base + $75 mileage = $300 total.

## Stay Safe

- Stay in your vehicle if on busy roads
- Turn on hazard lights immediately  
- Move to shoulder or safe area if possible
- Keep doors locked until tow truck arrives
- Verify driver credentials and truck company name
- Don't leave valuables in the car during transport
- Get receipt with driver name and truck number
