> **Quick Answer:** Phoenix 24-hour towing typically costs $75-150 base fee plus $3-7 per mile. AAA members pay less. Expect higher rates during peak hours, weekends, and holidays. Always ask for total cost upfront before agreeing to service.

## What To Do

1. **Call your insurance first** - Many policies include roadside assistance that costs less than cash rates
2. **Check for AAA membership** - Saves $50-100 per call if you're already a member
3. **Get exact pricing upfront** - Ask for base fee, per-mile rate, and any additional charges
4. **Confirm your exact location** - Use GPS coordinates or nearby landmarks for faster service
5. **Ask for arrival time estimate** - Reputable companies provide realistic timeframes
6. **Have your vehicle information ready** - Make, model, year, and any special requirements

## What It Might Cost

**Standard 24-hour rates in Phoenix:**
- Base hook-up fee: $75-150
- Per-mile charge: $3-7
- Average 5-mile tow: $90-185 total

**Premium rates apply:**
- Nights (6 PM - 6 AM): Add 25-50%
- Weekends: Add $15-30
- Holidays: Add $25-50
- Heavy vehicles/motorcycles: Add $25-75

**AAA member rates:**
- Basic plan: 4 free tows up to 3 miles
- Plus plan: 4 free tows up to 100 miles
- Premier plan: 4 free tows up to 200 miles

## Stay Safe

- Stay with your vehicle until help arrives
- Keep doors locked, windows cracked for air
- Use hazard lights and reflective triangles if you have them
- Never accept rides from strangers offering help
- Verify the tow truck matches the company you called
- Get written receipt with all charges itemized
- Take photos of your car's condition before towing
