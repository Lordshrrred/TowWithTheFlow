> **Quick Answer:** Sunday towing in Chicago typically costs $75-150 base plus $3-7 per mile. Call your insurance first (many cover towing), then compare local services. AAA and some credit cards also provide discounted rates. Expect 30-60 minute wait times on Sundays.

## What To Do

1. **Check your coverage first**
   - Call your auto insurance company
   - Check if you have AAA membership
   - Look for credit card roadside benefits

2. **Get multiple quotes**
   - Call at least 3 local services
   - Ask for total estimated cost upfront
   - Confirm they can reach you within reasonable time

3. **Compare these Chicago-area services**
   - Chicago Towing: (312) 666-8697
   - Windy City Towing: (773) 583-9464
   - South Side Towing: (773) 778-8697
   - Lincoln Towing: (773) 935-3343

4. **Ask the right questions**
   - "What's your Sunday base rate?"
   - "How much per mile after the first 5?"
   - "Any extra fees for Sunday service?"
   - "How long until you arrive?"

5. **While you wait**
   - Turn on hazard lights
   - Move to passenger seat if on busy road
   - Keep doors locked until tow truck arrives

## What It Might Cost

**Standard Sunday rates in Chicago:**
- Base hook-up: $75-100
- Mileage: $3-7 per mile
- Sunday surcharge: $10-25 additional
- Total for 10-mile tow: $125-175

**Money-saving tips:**
- Insurance coverage can reduce cost to $0-50
- AAA members pay $0 for first 5 miles
- Some services waive Sunday fees for seniors

## Stay Safe

- Stay inside your vehicle if on highway
- Exit on traffic side only if absolutely necessary  
- Keep phone charged and share your location
- Have cash ready (some services prefer cash payments)
- Don't agree to repairs on the spot from tow driver
