> **Quick Answer:** AAA Basic costs $56-68/year but limits you to 4 calls and 3-7 mile tows. Private towing runs $75-200 per call depending on distance and time. AAA wins if you use it 2+ times yearly, but private gives unlimited distance and faster response times.

## What To Do

1. **Calculate your yearly towing needs.** If you call for help twice or more per year, AAA membership pays for itself.

2. **Check AAA limitations first.** Basic membership covers only 3-5 miles (varies by region). Plus membership covers 10 miles, Premier covers 100 miles.

3. **Get private towing quotes immediately.** Call 2-3 local companies for current rates. Prices vary wildly by location and time of day.

4. **Factor in wait times.** AAA often takes 45-90 minutes. Private companies usually arrive in 20-45 minutes, especially if you're blocking traffic.

5. **Consider your vehicle type.** AAA has weight limits. Heavy trucks, RVs, or modified vehicles need private towing anyway.

## What It Might Cost

**AAA Membership (Annual):**
- Basic: $56-68 (4 calls, 3-7 miles each)
- Plus: $91-119 (4 calls, 10 miles each)  
- Premier: $119-144 (4 calls, 100 miles each)

**Private Towing (Per Call):**
- Local tow (under 10 miles): $75-125
- Medium distance (10-50 miles): $125-200
- Long distance: $2-4 per mile beyond base rate
- After-hours surcharge: $25-50 extra
- Highway/dangerous location: $50-75 extra

**Break-even point:** Two AAA calls per year equals private towing costs in most markets.

## Stay Safe

- Never agree to towing without getting the total price upfront
- Ask about storage fees at the destination
- Private companies can negotiate rates, AAA rates are fixed
- Keep cash or card ready for private towing payment
- AAA membership includes other services (battery, lockout, fuel delivery)
- Some insurance policies include towing coverage, check before paying extra
